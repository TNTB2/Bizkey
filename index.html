SERVICES



All encompassing accounting solutions


Bizkey strives to provide clients with the most comprehensive business tools and services.

These tools and services are customisable to suit your specific business needs. A complete function of financial services, forms part of this service and are set out as below:

Accounting and Bookkeeping

  • Bookkeeping (both monthly and ad-hoc)
  • Management reporting
  • Capture cashbook on accounting software
  • Debtors reconciliation
  • Creditors reconciliation
  • Bank reconciliations
  • Monthly Income Statements
  • Balance Sheets

Payroll

  • Once off salary structuring (per employee)
  • Pay slips (per employee, monthly)
  • EMP201 monthly submissions
  • EMP501 bi-annual submissions
Note: This service includes the monthly calculation of salaries and the preparation of pay slips. Pay slips can be emailed monthly to employees or printed copies provided. A summary of the payroll is provided to management.

Registrations

  • Registration of new businesses
  • SARS tax registrations
  • CIPC Registrations
  • Director/Member Amendments
  • Annual Returns
